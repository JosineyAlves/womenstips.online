Check out this recipe for a bacon-wrapped meatloaf stuffed with cheese, which is not only delicious but will also surprise everyone at lunch or dinner!

Ingredients:

  • 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1 cup grated cheese
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 200g chopped bacon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 300°F (150°C).
  2. Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  3. Chop the bacon and fry in a skillet until crispy.
  4. In a bowl, mix the almond flour and baking powder.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine the wet and dry ingredients with the melted butter. Stir well.
  6. Add the bacon and grated cheese, forming a dough-like mixture.
  7. Place the mixture in a baking pan and bake for about 45 minutes.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool before serving.
